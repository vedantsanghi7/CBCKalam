Events


  • NHA organised National Level Workshop on AB PM-JAY at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi on Jan 17, 2025
  • Ayushman Sangam (North Eastern Region) - NHA organized a two-day regional review meeting for eight north eastern states for PMJAY on 16th and 17th March 2023.
  • NHA organised a two-day comprehensive training workshop for railways hospitals for providing quality healthcare to PM-JAY Beneficiaries.
  • Global Conference on Digital Health - NHA was directed by the Ministry to set-up stall for PM-JAY and participate as a speaker at the Gloabl Conference on Digital Health being organized by WHO in New Delhi on 20th and 21st March 2023.
  • Inauguration of Arogya Manthan 2022 by Shri. Mansukh Mandaviya Ji, Hon’ble Union Minister for Health & Family Welfare on 23rd September 2022.
